Plan a business event or conference

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Business events are a catalyst for social and economic transformation, drivers of change, and a forum for new ideas. When you host your business event in Scotland you will be inspired by our pioneering spirit, our commitment to equality, and our vision for a better future for all.

Scotland is the perfect fit for your next business event

Scotland is an incredible destination for business events. A destination that fights against climate change, values thriving communities, embraces innovation and has sustainable development at its heart. The  VisitScotland Business Events team is here to help you make the most of what Scotland has to offer. 

Picking the right destination is critical to planning a successful and memorable event. Scotland has lots of fantastic destinations and venues to choose from and is a country bursting with options to enjoy away from the conference. Once delegates experience Scotland, they cannot wait to come back.

Scotland has seven distinctly different cities and regions. Each of these is home to a diverse range of events venues – from vast conference centres that can handle large-scale international events, to unique places that offer luxury experiences. 

Outside our cities, you’ll find show-stopping venues, such as castles and country estates in glorious surroundings, that are perfect for incentive activities. With award-winning hospitality, quality food and drink, and world-renowned places of industry and academic learning, Scotland is an ideal destination for business events. 

With industry-leading businesses covering technology, finance, space, energy, sustainability, and food and drink, Scotland has world leading experts in many sectors able to support your event.

Find out how your event can achieve more in Scotland, create new connections and make change that lasts.
