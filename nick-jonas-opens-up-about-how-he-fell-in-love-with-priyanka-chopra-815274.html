Nick Jonas Opens Up About How He Fell in Love with Priyanka Chopra

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 23, 2024

In 2000, during the Miss World pageant’s glamorous spectacle, Priyanka Chopra became Miss World. Across the world, Nick Jonas was just a 7-year-old boy, unaware of his future destiny.

As Priyanka won, Nick’s mother, Denise, watched with anticipation. Little did she know that this moment would set the stage for a legendary love story.

Full of love and pride, Denise held her son, unaware of his future with Priyanka. With innocent eyes, Young Nick would later win Miss World’s heart.

Denise cherished the memory of that day, never imagining it would become a part of her family’s story—a tale resembling a timeless fairy tale.

He fell in love with her long before they would meet.

In a heartwarming episode of The Jennifer Hudson Show, Priyanka Chopra shared a remarkable twist of fate that connected her life with her husband, Nick Jonas, long before they officially met.

It all started with a surprising revelation from her mother-in-law, Denise Miller-Jonas, who shared a momentous day in 2000 when Priyanka was crowned Miss World, launching her Bollywood and later Hollywood career.

This revelation was magical because, at the time of Priyanka’s crowning, Nick Jonas was a 7-year-old boy, unaware of their future connection. As Priyanka shared this astonishing anecdote, the audience gasped in amazement, and Jennifer Hudson was visibly moved.

The idea that Nick’s family had watched Priyanka on TV over two decades ago without realizing she would become a cherished family member was a fairytale come to life. It underscored that life often has a grander plan, unfolding unexpectedly.

As Priyanka continued, it became clear that her love story with Nick was beautiful and a testament to the idea that true love transcends time and circumstance. It was a reminder that destinies can be woven together long before we realize it, resulting in a love that is nothing short of magical.

He knew she was the one.

Jonas believed he and Chopra were meant to be when he saw her enter the Chateau Marmont in Los Angeles during one of their first dates. “She walks into the Chateau, and I feel an overwhelming sense of peace and understanding about this next chapter of my life,” he said.
A few days later, he enthusiastically called his mother to share his plans to marry her.

The two find each other most attractive in the morning.

Chopra mentioned that Jonas finds her most beautiful in the mornings, right after she wakes up.

“It’s actually really annoying, but he insists on looking at my face when I wake up. I’m just like, ’Wait one minute. Let me just get a little mascara on, let me put on my moisturizer.’ I’m sleepy-eyed face right now, but he’s just ... it’s amazing and super sweet,” she shared.

When asked when Chopra thinks Jonas looks most attractive, she responded, “All the time.”

Love can be written in the stars.

Priyanka Chopra shared her thoughts on the mysterious force that united her with Nick Jonas in a heartfelt moment. When asked if their relationship was destined, she replied with a contemplative “Maybe,” conveying reverence for their journey together.

She believed in fate and human connections, stating, “I do believe that people are meant to be with each other for whatever time that is meant to be.” This perspective recognized that love comes in various forms, with meaningful moments shaping our lives.

Priyanka emphasized that people come into each other’s lives for a reason: to create lasting memories.

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas’s love story is a powerful example of love’s enduring magic. Their journey from strangers to soulmates, overcoming obstacles to build a life together, warms our hearts and restores our faith in love.

In a world that can seem cynical, their story offers hope. It shows that love can conquer all, transcending boundaries and thriving unexpectedly. True love is worth the fight, capable of weathering storms and emerging stronger.

Amid life’s challenges, their love story brings hope. It reaffirms our belief in love’s existence and its ability to bridge differences and ignite deep affection. It proves that love can unite people, regardless of their backgrounds.

Their story reminds us of true love’s timeless and transformative power, melting our hearts and making us believe against all odds.
