Canada’s Got Talent is returning this month with a Million Dollar Season, as over 100 acts will compete to win $1 million courtesy of Rogers. The season features performers from all around Canada, as well as several from abroad — representing singers, dancers, magicians, and more.

Who’s Competing on Canada’s Got Talent Season 4?

The acts competing on this season of CGT were shared in a press release. You can learn more about each of the acts on the Citytv website. You might recognize past Got Talent acts such as Nicolas Ribs, Duo Desire, and Funkanometry.

Golden Buzzer Acts to Receive Prizes

In addition to upping the grand prize to $1 million, this season of CGT will also award prizes to the acts who earn the Golden Buzzer. Each recipient of the coveted honor will receive a $25,000 prize, as well as moving straight to the Semifinals.

The show’s judges (Howie Mandel, Lilly Singh, Trish Stratus, and Kardinal Offishall) are returning to the panel for Season 4, along with host Lindsay Ell. Fans can watch the two-hour premiere on Tuesday, March 19 on Citytv. It will also be streaming on Citytv+.
