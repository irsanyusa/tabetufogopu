Austin City Limits: Radiohead to Headline ACL Music Festival

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

May 5, 2016 11:07 PM EDT

Radiohead will headline the Austin City Limits Music Festival this year. The iconic British rock band, which recently released a new single, will perform alongside Mumford & Sons, Kendrick Lamar and LCD Soundsystem.

The festival will roll over two autumn weekends, September 30 to October 2, and October 7 to 9, as it celebrates its 15th anniversary.
