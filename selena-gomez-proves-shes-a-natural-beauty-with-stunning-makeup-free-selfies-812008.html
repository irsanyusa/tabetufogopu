Selena Gomez Proves Shes a Natural Beauty With Stunning Makeup-Free Selfies

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 24, 2024

Dermatologists claim that going makeup-free can make your skin more oily and your pores more clogged. However, it’s only a temporary state, as your skin is recalibrating and getting used to a new life. Selena Gomez is an example of a woman who’s not afraid to wipe off all that concealer, eyeshadow, mascara, and glitter to reveal her true and natural self. And she’s getting the highest praise for this both from her fans and colleagues who have already prepared a bunch of compliments for her sweet look.

Selena Gomez’s new makeup-free selfies made a real splash on Instagram.

Selena Gomez went makeup-free again, and she started the week with 2 Instagram selfies that showed her radiant complexion.

The 30-year-old superstar absolutely rocked a black low-cut tank top as she pleased her 398 million followers with the pair of natural and candid photos.

Gomez appeared with her dark hair arranged in casual waves, and she appeared to be really enjoying some free time at home, looking relaxed.

People are endlessly praising Gomez for being herself.

“Violet chemistry,” the singer wrote alongside her makeup-free post. The photos earned rave reviews from her huge fanbase, racking up more than 13,000,000 likes in less than half a day.

The comments are all very sweet, and people seem to be truly amazed by Gomez’s radiant look. “Absolutely breathtaking gorgeous,” gushed fitness influencer Whitney Simmons, while another fan wrote, “The queen is queening.”

Gomez is a real fan of natural beauty.

Gomez always keeps it real with her followers and posts makeup-free selfies regularly to promote body positivity.

The star posted another very candid picture recently, where she ditched filters to proudly display a prominent blemish on her upper lip.

“Me,” wrote the new queen of Instagram, posting honest photos and gaining absolute praise in her comment section.
