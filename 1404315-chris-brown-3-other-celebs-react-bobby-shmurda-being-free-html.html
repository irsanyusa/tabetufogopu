Chris Brown and 3 other celebs react to Bobby Shmurda being free from prison

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

- Hip-hop fans around the world have awaited the release of Bobby Shmurda for years and it has finally happened

- Celebrities and colleagues of the rapper joined in the celebration of his release

- Legit.ng has compiled a list of superstars who reacted to the news and wished Bobby well

American rapper Bobby Shmurda has been released from prison and celebrities all over the world are celebrating. The 26-year-old rapper pleaded guilty to one count of third-degree conspiracy and one count of weapon possession back in 2016.

Bobby was released from prison on February 23, 2021, and fans welcomed him back with open arms. Legit.ng takes a look at four celebrities who reacted to Bobby's Shmurda's release.

1. Chris Brown

American singer and dancer Chris Brown took to his Instagram stories to celebrate Bobby's release. He even referred to him as the King of New York. Breezy was very loud and proud about Bobby's return on social media.

2. Quavo

Migos rapper Quavo was open about supporting Bobby as well. The rapper even said that he would personally pick up Bobby from prison himself. Quavo posted a video of Bobby getting off a plane and he was there to welcome him, along with a group of other friends.

3. Meek Mill

Meek has got himself into trouble after rapping about Kobe Bryant's helicopter crashbut he took to Instagram to share how happy he is that Bobby has been released. Meek captioned his post:

"Welcome home Bobby, turn this all the way up."
