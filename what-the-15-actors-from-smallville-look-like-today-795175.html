What the 15 Actors From Smallville Look Like Today

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024

When Tom Welling auditioned for the role of Clark Kent, a young Superman, he didn’t know anything about his character and wasn’t a big fan of comic books. But he managed to grab viewers’ attention for 10 full seasons and plausibly show the transformation from an ordinary boy to a real superhero. Even 18 years later after the first episode was released, we’re still thrilled by all the dark secrets from the town of Smallville and the incredible love story of Clark Kent and Lois Lane.

We at Bright Side admire Smallville’s success and we got curious about how our favorite actors changed after the final season. At the end of the article, you’ll find a piece of exciting news that will please the true fans of Smallville.

Tom Welling (Clark Kent)

Michael Rosenbaum (Lex Luthor)

Kristin Kreuk (Lana Lang)

Sam Jones III (Pete Ross)

Allison Mack (Chloe Sullivan)

Erica Durance (Lois Lane)

Justin Hartley (Oliver Queen)

Jensen Ackles (Jason Teague)

John Glover (Lionel Luthor)

John Schneider (Jonathan Kent)

Annette O’Toole (Martha Kent)

Laura Vandervoort (Kara)

Cassidy Freeman (Tess Mercer)

Aaron Ashmore (Jimmy Olsen)

Eric Johnson (Whitney Fordman)

Bonus: Clark Kent and Lois Lane are back together again!

Erica and Tom got back together to work on the CW’s DC Arrowverse crossover “Crisis on Infinite Earths.” So we finally have a chance to find out what Superman has been doing since the Smallville finale and whether his love for Lois Lane is still going strong after 8 years.

Are you a Smallville fan? Which character is your favorite? Tell us in the comment section!
