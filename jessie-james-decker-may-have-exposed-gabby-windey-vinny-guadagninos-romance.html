Jessie James Decker May Have Exposed Gabby Windey, Vinny Guadagnino's Romance

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 1, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

DWTS Tour

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Vinny Guadagnino and Gabby Windey have fueled relationship rumors with their flirtatious social media discussions after meeting as Dancing With the Stars contestants. Their fellow season 31 competitor Jesse James Decker may have spilled the beans about the rumored romance between the two.

Jessie James Decker Referred to Guadagnino as Windey’s “Honey”

The duo of Windey and Guadagnino kept a professional relationship during live shows on DWTS. Though they kept their personal relationship out of the public eye during the show, that all changed when they met again on the DWTS tour.

Rumors that Windey and Guadagnino had taken their relationship to the next level started to spread when they got back together for a few tour dates in the northeast. The playful and flirty banter they have on social media also has got the attention of fans that suspect them of currently dating each other.

On one of Windey’s Instagram posts showing snaps from the DWTS tour, Decker left a comment that seemingly confirms the romance. The singer mentioned Guadagnino in her comment, referring to him as Windey’s “Honey.”

“Dancing with your honey Vinny Guadagnino can’t wait to double date in Nash,” Decker wrote.

The pictures from the post show Windey onstage together with Guadagnino along with their DWTS castmates. The comment got everyone’s attention, and quickly became the most liked and replied comment on the post. Fans quickly asked Decker if her comment was a confirmation of the two dating, but the singer has yet to respond.

Windey Said She Would Go On a Date With Guadagnino

The chemistry between Windey and Guadagnino is highly obvious and the two have even said they would date each other. As far as opening up about their relationship, Windey said that they’re just “having fun,” as close friends with the possibility of becoming something more.

“We formed a friendship right away and I feel like our senses of humor are really similar,” Windey said.

As for Guadagnino, he revealed in an interview recently that he would definitely go on a date with the former Bachelorette. He expressed that the chance hasn’t presented itself as of yet, with the biggest possibility at the DWTS tour.

“I would take her on a date one day, there it is. We’re both comedians so that’s where you get a lot of the banter.” Guadagnino said. “She’s a comedian, I’m a comedian, but I think that we do have good chemistry and I would take her on a date.”
