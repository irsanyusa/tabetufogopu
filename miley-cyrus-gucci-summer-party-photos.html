From Miley Cyrus Singing Flowers to Kim Petras Losing Her Gold Bracelet in a Pool: Insid

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

Miley Cyrus made a rare public appearance Thursday night to co-host Gucci’s summer celebration party at Chateau Marmont in West Hollywood with the Italian luxury house’s creative director Sabato De Sarno.

“It’s been a long time,” the singer told Variety as she entered the party, “I actually left the house.”

Cyrus, the face of Gucci Flora perfumes, gave a poolside performance of a jazzy rendition of her hit, “Flowers.” At one point, she paused to ask the crowd, “Why am I singing and you’re not?” before cracking, “Is that Kendall Jenner doing harmonies?”

In addition to Jenner, other guests included Salma Hayek, Chloe Kim, Rumer Willis, Finneas, Mark Foster, David LaChapelle, Donovan Leitch, Soo Joo Park, Brandi Cyrus, Noah Cyrus, Tish Cyrus and Dominic Purcell.

The well-dressed crowd arrived through a rose garden tunnel inspired by the new Gucci Flora Gorgeous Orchid Eau de Parfum campaign, shot by photographer Tyler Mitchell. The drink menu included cocktails with playful names like “Sparking Personality” and “Strawberry Moon Mocktail.” DJ Alex Chapman provided the music.

Kim Petras looked chic in a tennis dress and heels, but had a bit of fashion scare when her gold bracelet fell into the pool. Hotel staff, as seen on actor Andrew Matarazzo’s Instagram, quickly came to her aide with pool skimmers to retrieve it.
