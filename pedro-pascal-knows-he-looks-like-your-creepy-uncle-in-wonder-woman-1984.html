Wonder Woman 1984 will be released on June 5, 2020. The sequel will follow Wonder Woman as she comes across two villains, Pascal's Maxwell Lord and the Cheetah, played by Kristen Wiig . The movie is set during the height of the Cold War .

As the DC Wiki page explains, "Maxwell Lord is a wealthy businessman and entrepreneur responsible for organizing the Justice League International. Taking an interest in their management, he secured United Nations sponsorship for the League and acted as their leader for several years. During Invasion! he discovered he was a metahuman with powerful mind control abilities. He eventually betrayed the Justice League International, killed Ted Kord [Blue Beetle] and attempted to eliminate all metahumans. He has also been a member of the Arcana, the Black Lantern Corps, Checkmate, the Extremists, the Illuminati and the Super Buddies."
