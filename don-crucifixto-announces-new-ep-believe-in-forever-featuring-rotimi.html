Don Crucifixto announces new EP 'Believe in Forever' featuring Rotimi

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 25, 2024

The 4-track EP promises to captivate listeners with its fusion of Afrobeat, R&B, and contemporary sounds, showcasing Don Crucifixto's versatile artistry and unique musical vision. Highlighting the EP is a standout collaboration with internationally Hollywood singer and actor Rotimi.

Known for his work on the hit TV series "Power" and his chart-topping music career, Rotimi's guest appearance adds a dynamic layer to the project, blending his distinctive style with Don Crucifixto's rich musicality. Don Reveals that the music video for their collaborative track "I Believe" is set to drop on the 2 August 2024.

"Believe in Forever" is a testament to Don Crucifixto's journey as an artist and a storyteller. Each track offers a deep dive into themes of love, resilience, and the enduring human spirit, encapsulated through infectious rhythms and heartfelt lyrics. The Tracklist includes; Summer Time: An electrifying opening that sets the tone for the EP, evoking the vibrant and carefree essence of summer. I Believe (featuring Rotimi): A vibrant collaboration that fuses the best of both artists' styles, delivering a powerful message of hope and belief.

No Competition: A soulful and assertive anthem that delves into themes of self-confidence and personal triumph. Forever: An upbeat and joyous closing track celebrating the timeless nature of love and lifeâ€™s enduring moments. While talking about the EP, Don Crucifixto expressed his excitement and gratitude: "Creating 'Believe in Forever" has been an incredible journey.

Collaborating with Rotimi was a dream come true, and I'm thrilled to share this music with the world. I hope these songs inspire and resonate with everyone who listens." Don Crucifixto, who is also the record label owner of Don Crucifixto Entertainment, continues to build on the success of his previous album "Misunderstood," which has garnered over 10 million combined streams.
