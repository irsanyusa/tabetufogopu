20 Celebs Who Are the Spitting Images of Other People

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

With so many celebrities around, sometimes it’s hard to keep up with who’s who. However, that task gets a million times harder once you realize so many of them share mind-blowing resemblances with each other.

With that in mind, Bright Side made this list of celebrities who could very well be related to each other, given how much they look alike. Feel free to use it as a guide, but be warned it will leave you puzzled for a while.

1. Margot Robbie and Sophia Loren

2. Amy Adams and Tonya Harding

3. James Dean and James Franco

4. Robert Redford and Brad Pitt

5. Christian Bale and James Brolin

6. Rachel McAdams and Paolla Oliveira

7. Evanna Lynch and Emily Kinney

8. Skylar Astin and Dane Cook

9. Sophie Turner and Boy George

10. Carla Bruni and Bella Hadid

11. Tom Hardy and Logan Marshall-Green

12. Richard Madden and Gus Kenworthy

13. Will Ferrell and Chad Smith

14. Idina Menzel and Lea Michele

15. Lili Reinhart and Sarah Gadon

16. Amanda Peet and Lake Bell

17. Julie Bowen and Elizabeth Banks

18. Paul Reubens and Alan Cumming

19. Evan Rachel Wood and Rachel Brosnahan

20. Jai Courtney and Phillip Phillips

Which of these pairs surprised you the most with their resemblance? And which do you think could be a great addition to this list? Feel free to strike up a conversation with us in the comments because we always love to hear your thoughts on our articles!
