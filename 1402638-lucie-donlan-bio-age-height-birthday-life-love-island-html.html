Lucie Donlan bio: age, height, birthday, life after Love Island

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Lucie Donlan is a British surfer, model, and reality television personality. She is most recognized for having been a contestant on ITV's Love Island in its fifth season in 2019. She is one of youngest ISA Surf Judges in the UK.

Lucie is also an influential social media star and vlogger. What else is known about this ambitious young lady?

  • Full name: Lucie Rose Donlan
  • Lucie Donlan birthday: February 22, 1998
  • Age: 23 (as of 2021)
  • Career: Surfer, model, reality TV star
  • Nationality: British
  • Height: 5ft 5 inches
  • Weight: 55kg
  • Instagram: @lucierosedonlan

Lucie Donlan biography

Lucie was born on February 22, 1998. She was born and raised in Newquay, Cornwell, UK. She has two siblings, an elder brother and a younger sister named Emily.

She developed an interest in surfing at an early age. She hence pursued professional training in that area, and her mom was of great support in this. She also has a loving extended family and is deeply bonded to her aunties and uncles.

Education

The famed TV personality attended Wadebridge School (2009 – 2014) and then joined Treviglas Surf Academy (2014 – 2016).

Career

Lucie Donlan began surfing at the age of 13 and was involved in competitive surfing in her early teen years. She has worked as an ISA Surf Coach and an ISA Surf Judge, working with Surfing England and the UK Pro Surf Tour. She has also been an ambassador for The Wave Project, a surf therapy charity that helps young people deal with anxiety and gain confidence through surfing.

The passionate surfer is also a model, an area she started exploring when she was seventeen years old. She signed with a London modeling agency and has since modeled for major publications and various high profile campaigns.

The glamorous 23-year-old has been an ambassador for Fistral Beach Hotel and Spa and has appeared in High Tide, Coast, and Wed magazines. She was also featured in the film Night Rose.
