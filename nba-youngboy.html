NBA YoungBoy (Rapper) - On This Day

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 30, 2024

Full Name: Kentrell DeSean Gaulden
Profession: Rapper

Biography: NBA YoungBoy, also known as YoungBoy Never Broke Again, is an American rapper that came to fame with a series of mixtapes that led to a cult following. He was then signed to Atlantic Records, releasing his first hit single "Outside Today" (2018) and his debut studio album Until Death Call My Name (2018).

His mixtape AI YoungBoy 2 (2019) debuted at number one of the Billboard 200, and his mixtape 38 Baby 2 (2020) also topped this chart as did his second studio album, Top (2020).

Notably, his third studio album Sincerely, Kentrell (2021) was released during a period of incarceration for a federal firearms charge. The success of this album made him the third artist, after 2Pac and Lil Wayne, to have a number one album released from prison.

Born: October 20, 1999
Birthplace: Baton Rouge, Louisiana, USA
Age: 24 years old

Generation: Generation Z
Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
Star Sign: Libra
